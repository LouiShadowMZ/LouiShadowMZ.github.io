SINENDRAMALA

One should approach an astrologer with such a devotion and affection similar to a devotee of God and the pet child of a mother. Unless one is sincere, even Veda is not useful to him. Whenever one approaches a manthravadi, or doctor or an astrologer, one should never go empty handed.

Scars Sun, Mars, Mercury and Jupiter give marks on the right side, whereas Moon, Venus, Saturn and Nodes cause moles and marks on the left side.

Cancer and Scorpio indicate reptiles: Capricorn and Pisces show birds. Other signs denote two-legged or four-legged creations of God. Moon and Nodes indicate the reptiles and also those who do not have legs; Mercury indicates birds; others denote human beings and animals. Saturn indicates lame or fractured or amputated leg.

Aries and Sagittarius denote forest; Taurus agricultural land; Gemini and Virgo-village: Cancer-canal; Leo-Hill and Mountain; Libra and Capricorn river; Scorpio-well; Aquarius-water.

Aries-forest; Taurus agricultural land; Gemini village: Cancer-canal: Leo-hill: Virgo-water; Libra-village; Scorpio-well; Sagittarius-garden; Capricorn-dry canal: Aquarius-lake; and Pisces Ocean.

Sun, Mars and Saturn forest; Moon, Venus--water: Mer- cury, Jupiter-village; Nodes-bush: Sun and Jupiter-pooja room: Moon and Venus--water; Mars carth; Mercury-resting place; Saturn--the area where religious functions are done, or kitchen; Nodes the hole in the tree, or anywhere there is the hole.

Saturn and Nodes indicate one year; Sun denotes six months; Mercury two months; Jupiter one month; Vemus 15 days; Mars one day and Moon 1-hours. This is to find out the time needed to materialise.

﻿Plantation: Sun-trees; Mars-produce from dry land; Mercury and Venus-produce from wet land: Jupiter and Venus sugarcane and same species; Saturn and Nodes thorny trees.

Leo and Sagittarius-trees: Taurus, Cancer and Libra-creepers: Aries and Scorpio-produce from dry land; Pisces- sugarcane; Gemini and Virgo plants without thorn, Capricorn and Aquarius thorny trees.

Sun-trees in the forest: Moon-planting trees; Jupiter- Coconut trees; Saturn and Nodes palm trees; Mercury and. Venus domestic garden; Mars plants etc., Saturn indicates ginger: Safron and such bulbs which grow under the ground.

Sun indicates barks; Moon-bulbs; Mars-flowers; Mercury leaf: Jupiter and Venus-fruit: Saturn-root; and Nodes creepers.

Sun, Moon and Saturn indicate dry trees and others show fruit-bearing ones. The colour of the fruit will be similar to the planet. Sun indicates beans (wheat); Moon-white sesame; Mars dhall; Mercury-green dhall; Jupiter-Bengalgram; Venus all white ones and Saturn--sesame; Nodes black dhall, horsegram, etc.

Sun indicates tiger; Moon-milking animals; Mars-goat; Mercury-rabit; Jupiter-horse; Venus-cow: Saturn and Nodes buffaloes.

If Sun and Jupiter signify the animal, then it will be preg nant. Moon and Venus indicate that they will give plenty of milk, Saturn and Nodes indicate that they have no calves. No more issue. Mars indicates that they are dry and also cunning.

Thus Horary Astrology has been described in this book and various other rules which are found in Sinendhramala are similar to those already furnished in this book. Those which are not found in the other text-books had been published for the benefit of the readers.

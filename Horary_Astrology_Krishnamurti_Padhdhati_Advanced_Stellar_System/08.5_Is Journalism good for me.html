Is Journalism good for me

Third house represents Journalism. Jupiter and Mercury are the two planets which indicate Journalism.

 

According to Westerners the good aspects between Moon. Mercury and Jupiter indicate that it is favourable for journalism.

 

Virgo and Pisces are said to be the signs favourable.

 

According to KRISHNAMURTI PADHDHATI if one has the desire to become a Journalist and he puts the query, one has to judge whether the sub-lord of the 11th cusp (indicating his success) is deposited in the constellation of a planet which is the significator of the houses 3 and 9 and if the sub-lord is in any manner connected with Jupiter or Mercury or both. If the sub-lord happens to be Venus, one will be writing much about the marriage, sex, love affairs, Chemistry etc. But if is Jupiter, most sensible and useful articles will be written and the books published will be popular. Considering the natural quality of the planet one has to fix the subject of the book which one will write.

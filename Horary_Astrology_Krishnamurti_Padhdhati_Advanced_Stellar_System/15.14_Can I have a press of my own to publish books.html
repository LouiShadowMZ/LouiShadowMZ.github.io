Can I have a press of my own to publish books

Press is indicated by Mars, Mercury and Jupiter. Therefore, if the sub-lord of the meridian is deposited in the constellation of a planet which is the significator of at least one of the three houses 2, 6 and 10 and also if it is in any manner connected with Mars and Mercury, one will have one's own press.

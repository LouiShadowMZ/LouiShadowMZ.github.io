SIGNS AND PLACES

Aries: Sandy or pasture ground, small hill there where thieves stay, stables.

Taurus: Cow-shed, cattle-shed, furniture. pasture ground away from the house, agricultural land.

Gemini: Boxes, trunks, hills, mountains, playing grounds, schools, nurseries, libraries.

Cancer: Rivers, lakes, sea, springs, marshy area.

Leo: Forest, desert, rock, fort, castles, palaces, furnaces, ovens, kitchens.

Virgo Libraries, book-shelves, books, closets, corns fields.

Libra: Out-house, bed-room, inner-chamber, wind-mill, top of the mountain, side of the hill, well ventilated place.

Scorpio: Drains, cesspools, ruins, marshy area, washbasin, laundry and places where rubbish is thrown.

Sagittarius: Stable, hill, high land, pooja-room, temple, cash-box, money-purse.

Capricorn Cow-shed, lumber-room, store-room, thorny places, low lying area.

Aquarius: Mines, metals, quarries, conduits, tunnels, lecture room, or research laboratories.

Pisces: Wells, rivers, ponds, fish, water-mill. pumps, cisterns.

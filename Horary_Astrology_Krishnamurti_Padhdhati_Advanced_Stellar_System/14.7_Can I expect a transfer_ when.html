For transfer in service we have to judge the houses 3, 10 and 12 because the 3rd house shows change of place and short journey, 10th shows employment and the 12th shows altogether new circumstances,

 

To proceed on leave or to go on tour, the houses3, 9 and 12 are to be considered.

 

According to K. P. the owner of a house is the owner of the sign where the cusp falls. Planets in the constellation of the owner of a House are stronger than the owner to give the results of that house. Further the occupant of a bhava is stronger than the planets in the constellations of the owner of the house and the planets in the constellations of the occupants are stronger than the occupants. All these planets will, how ever, give the results of that house, but in a varying degree as above.

 

Rahu and Kethu are nodes. They are always stronger than the planets represented by them. They give firstly the results of the planet or planets with which they are conjoined, then the results of the planet in whose constellation they are deposited, then that of the planets aspecting them and then the results of the owner of the sign occupied by them.

 

The query is whether he can expect transfer and, if yes, when? This depends upon what dasa and bhukti he is running. One can expect transfer during the conjoined period of the signi- ficators of houses 3, 10 and 12. The significators of the houses 3, 10 and 12 are Moon, Mercury, Kethu, Venus, Saturn, Rahu and Mars. Whenever the significators are more in number the best method to select the beneficial ones and eliminate the tempters is to find out the ruling planets at the time of judgment. and see which of them are common with the significators.

 

Those ruling planets which are deposited in the constellation or sub of a retrograde planet will not read the result. Reject them and take the remaining.

 

To-day is Wednesday ruled by Mercury. The owners of the sign and star transited by Moon are Mars and Kethu respectively. The lagna is Thulam governed by Venus. As Rahu is associated with Venus take Rahu also. (Kethu is aspected by Venus and is posited in Mercury's sign. But it has already come as a ruling planet).

 

So the ruling planets are Mercury, Mars, Kethu, Venus and Rahu. None of them are posited either in the constellation or sub of a retrograde planet. All of them are common with the significators. So you can expect transfer during their conjoined period.

 

Mars is by itself retrograde. So it will not be able to give the results as long as it is retrograde. Afterwards it can give.

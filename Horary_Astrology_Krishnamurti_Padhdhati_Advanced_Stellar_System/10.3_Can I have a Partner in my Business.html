Can I have a Partner in my Business

If the sub-lord of the 8th cusp is situated in the constellation of a planet signifying houses 6 and 11, you can have a partner. But if the sub-lord of the 8th cusp is deposited in a constellation, the lord of which is the significator of the houses 5 and 12, never have a partner.

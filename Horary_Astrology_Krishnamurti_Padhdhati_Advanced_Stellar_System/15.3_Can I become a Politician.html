"Then say that you want to become a statesman."

 

"What is the difference between a politician and a statesman?"

 

"A politician acts in such a way always bearing in his or her mind the next election. But a statesman cares more for the welfare of the next generation. A politician will form a party and join such a party which come out successful whereas a stateman minds only the progress and prosperity of his country: so, a statesman will guide and direct his course of action, whereas a politician will yield, adjust to circumstances and be satisfied to drift."

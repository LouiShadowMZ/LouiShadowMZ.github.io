ZODIAC

Division of the Zodiac: The Zodiac is divided into twelve equal parts; each division is called a Solar mansion (or rasi, or sign.)

 

They are Aries Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius. Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces.

 

The Hindus have another division also. The Zodiac is divided into twenty-seven equal parts: each measures 13° 20' They commence from Aries 0° and end by Pisces 30. The 27 divisions are called Lunar Mansions (Nakshatras, Asterisms, Stars Constellations). They are called Aswini, Bharani. Karthikai, Rohini. ... Uthrapathrapada and Revathi,

WHAT EACH PLANET SIGNIFIES

Neptune can produce ideal people with religious bent of mind and psychic powers. It can also produce lethargic, lazy, irresponsible, and immoral people who will cheat others and be dishonest.

 

It indicates poets, anaethesists, chemists, detectives, liquids, oils, and those who work in oil field, gas, fish, sea, hospitals, jails, caves, submarine, etc.

 

Uranus indicates intelligent or eccentric people, fortune, violence, sudden changes, invention, novelty, harmony or divorce, astrologers, teachers, lecturers, sculptors, those who hypnotise, mesmerise, those who work in railways, banks, gas plants, asylum, dispensaries, research laboratories, aerodrome etc.

 

Saturn imaginative, cautious action, silence, reserved nature, patience, preserving, studious, sincere, faithful; or envious, jealousy, untruthful, lazy, liar, discontentment, dishonest; also those who work in woods, unfrequented places, old churches, delabitated buildings, cess pools, drains, sewerage.

 

Jupiter produces magnanimous, noble, honourable, true, reliable people, with ambition and hopeful disposition. Their dealings will ever be fair and their mind will be to help others. They are religious and god-fearing. They try to stick on to tradition. They lead a very happy family life with their children; they are charitable and sympathetic. Jupiter indicates judges, advocates, professors, priests, ministers, administrators, chancellors, civilians, bankers, revenue depts. etc; also those who work in palace, gardens, law courts, etc.; those who deal in honey, oil, silk, clothing, horse, domestic fowls etc.

 

Mars: Brave, violent, unyielding, taking chances, pleasure in fighting, over liberal, extravagant. It shows captains, soldiers, surgeons, chemists, barbers, butchers, thieves, smiths, tailors, carpenters, masons, etc. It also denotes those who work in fire, furnaces, ovens, or deal in blood, and so on.

 

Sun: produces liberal, noble, and sympathetic people, reliable, proud and generous. They are fond of dress, jewels, internal decorations, and also outside; those who work in the Government in any capacity, magistrates, stewards, goldsmiths, copper-smiths, mint, municipalities, corporation, etc.

 

Venus: Calm, level headed, eversmiling, pleasing, never irritating, sweet, cheerful, dance, music, silk, linen. paint jewel, embroidery women, perfumery, engraving, bed, dance, banqueting hall. industrious,

 

Mercury: Intelligent, logician, studious, research minded, witty, learning without an instructor, travellers, good knowledge in occult sciences, astrologers, mathematicians, travelling agents, poets, advocates, dress, printers, publishers, orators, ambassadors. clerks, messengers, schools, assemblies, libraries.

 

Moon: Ever changing habits, unsteady, thinks of the present alone, timid, tries to lead a peaceful life, tries to avoid anxiety, worry, etc.: traveller, fish, breweries, milk, messengers, marine, navy, those who deal in liquids. (They do not take Rahu or Kethu.)

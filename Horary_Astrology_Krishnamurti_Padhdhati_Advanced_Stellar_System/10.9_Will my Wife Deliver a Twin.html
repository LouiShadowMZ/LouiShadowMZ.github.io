Will my Wife Deliver a Twin

If the sub-lord of the 11th cusp is deposited in the constellation of a planet which is either Mercury or which occupies any of the dual signs, Gemini, Sagittarius or Pisces then one's wife will deliver more than one at a time. Just like we see in the Horary Chart, it is also advisable to look to the number of the conception which she has, and then calculate, find out which house we have to judge. The cusp of that particular house should be ruled by a planet, which is deposited in the constellation of one who is either Mercury or who is deposited in one of the three duel signs.

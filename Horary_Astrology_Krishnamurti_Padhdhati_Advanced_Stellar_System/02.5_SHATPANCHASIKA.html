SHATPANCHASIKA

By PRITHUYASAS

Son of Varahamihira

CHAPTER 1

Sloka 1: Prithuyasas prays to the Sun and then for the benefit of Astrologers writes this work titled Shatpanchasika.

Sloka 2: The lagna rising at the moment of the query indicates changes in one's position or rank or transfer from the permanent place: the fourth house indicates success in one's attempts or progress. The tenth house shows the stay outside one's residence. 7th house indicates one's return, if the lagna or Navamsa Lagna were to be Seershalaya. But if the lagna is occupied by a malefic or if the navamsa lagna is a Prishtodaya one cannot be successful in his attempt. If the lagna or navamsa lagna is connected with benefics and also malefics. one will come out successful after facing difficulties. The planetary positions at the time of judgment should be studied. One will return to his permanent place when any planet enters into the fourth house.

Sloka 3: If the lord of a Bhava either occupies or aspects its own Bhava or if a Bhava is occupied by any benefic, it is auspicious in the results of that Bhava. But if a Bhava is occupied or aspected by any malefic the results of the Bhava will be undesirable. This principle is applicable both to the birth chart and also to the Horary (Prasna) one.

Sloka 4: An attempt will be successful if (a) a benefic occupies the lagna, (b) the navamsa lagna belongs to a benefic, (c) the lagna or navamsa lagna were to be Seershodaya. But if the lagna is occupied by a malefic or if the navamsa lagna is owned by an evil planet or if the lagna or navamsa lagna is Prishtodaya, one cannot be successful in his attempt. But if both benefics and malefics are connected with the lagna or navamsa lagnas, one will come out successful after facing difficulties.

Sloka 5: Strong Moon occupying the Lagna and also receiving aspect from a benefic Jupiter or Venus, promises the recovery of the lost property. Such beneficial result can be had if a strong benefic is deposited in the 11th house.

Sloka 6: If a planet is in its own navamsa and aspects its own in any of the Trikona houses, it means that the consultant wants to know about some minerals. If a planet which aspects does not occupy his own, the query will be about animals. If the planet which aspects is not in it is own navamsa but aspect the navamsa in the Trikona sign. the question will be about vegetable or agriculture.

Sloka 7: Alternate signs counted from Aries, are called masculine ones. The nine divisions of these odd signs indicate mineral, vegetable and animal kingdom in cyclic order. But in feminine signs, one is to reverse the order. So note whether the ascendant is masculine or feminine, find out the navamsa in which lagna rises and predict the nature of the query.

CHAPTER II

Sloka 1: If Taurus, Leo, Scorpio or Aquarius is the ascendant at the time of consultation, one will acquire a place or position. This does not indicate any journey and return to home; it does not indicate death or any loss. It does not show cure or failure (as the above signs are fixed ones).

Sloka 2: If the ascendant falls in a movable sign, just opposite results are to be expected. If the ascendant falls in a dual sign, the result will be mixed. If either the Lagna or the Moon receives aspects from benefics, one can predict success in one's attempts. If the ascendant receives aspects from evil planets, success is not promised.

Sloka 3 If evil planets are situated in the 5th or the 6th house, the opponent or the enemy will return home (it may be interpreted as follows: The opponent withdraws the case in the court). If the fourth house is occupied by malefic, the opponent will be defeated ultimately.

Sloka 4: If the fourth house be Cancer or Scorpio or Aquarius or Pisces the enemy will be defeated and he will return with his cavalry.

Sloka 5: Success to a person is to be predicted if the ascendant is in a movable sign, occupied by a benefic. Failure is to be expected if malefic occupies such an ascendant. If the ascendant is a fixed one, and evil planets occupy the ascendant, the result will be very advantageous.

Sloka 6: If the ascendant is in a movable sign and the Moon is in a fixed one, the arrival of the opponent is doubtful. But if Ascendant is in a fixed sign and Moon is in a movable one, the opponent will arrive.

Sloka 7: If the ascendant be in a fixed sign and the Moon is in a common one, the opponent will come a long way but retrace his own step.

Sloka 8: If the Moon be in a movable sign and the Ascendant be in a common one, the opponent comes half the way and returns home. If the Ascendant is in a movable sign and the Moon is deposited in a common one and both are aspected by malefics, there will be the fight (or war or litigation) and there will be loss for both.

Sloka 9: If the Ascendant is in a movable sign, and if either Sun or Saturn or Mercury or Venus be in the Ascendant, the opponent will march on speedily. But if Saturn, Mercury or Venus be retrograde the enemy will not leave his permanent place.

Sloka 10: If the Ascendant at the time of question be in a fixed sign and receives aspects from Jupiter and Saturn the opponent will not leave his place': but he will arrive, if the ascendant is in a fixed sign and is aspected by Jupiter and Saturn; and if evils are in three, five and six there will be the fight. Evil planets in the fourth house indicate that the opponent will retreat.

Sloka 11: If Sun and Moon are in the fourth house, the opponent will not come. But if Mercury, Jupiter and Venus are in the fourth house, the opponent will come quickly.

Sloka 12: If Aries, Taurus, Leo or Sagittarius happens to be the fourth house counted from the Ascendant, the enemy will return to his home whether these signs are occupied by planets or not.

Sloka 13: If Jupiter or Saturn were to be in the Ascendant, in a fixed sign the enemy would have left his place; he would have halted on his way; if movable sign is the Ascendant, and Sun or Jupiter were to be in the Ascendant, the enemy will surely arrive.

Sloka 14: Find the strongest planet and note in which house, it is deposited. One can expect the return of a person in so many months as is shown by the mumber of the house occupied by the strongest planet.

Sloka 15: If the strongest planet is situated in the Navamsa, in a movable sign, the above calculation is to be made. But if the navamsa sign is an immovable one, the duration is to be doubled. If the Navamsa sign is a common one, the period will be trebled.

Sloka 16: The return of a person is to be expected when the lord of the 7th house begins to retrograde (if Capricorn or Aquarius happens to be the ascendant this rule is not applicable at all).

Sloka 17: If there is no planet between the lagna and the sign occupied by Moon, the opponent will arrive within so many days as is indicated by the number of the sign occupied by Moon counted from the Ascendant.

CHAPTER III Success or Failure

Sloka 1: Even when a ruler of a place is opposed or attached by another, if either in the lagna or in the 7th or in the 10th house, benefits are deposited, one will win and come out successful. Jupiter, Venus and Mercury in the 9th house also brings victory. But Mars and Saturn in the 9th house threatens failure.

Sloka 2: The houses 3 to 8 counted from the Ascendant indicates the people in the place who are attacked if benefics are deposited in these houses; victory is certain for the people. The houses 9, 10, 11, 12 and 1, 2 if occupied by beneficial planets, there will be success. But, if in the 10th, 11th and 12th houses evil planets are deposited then the people in the place will lose whereas those who attacked will win.

Slokas 3 & 4: A compromise will be arrived at or a treaty will be the result if benefics occupy 1 or 11 or 12 and these houses happen to be two-legged-biped. If evil planets occupy common signs there will be war to the end. In 1 or 4 or 7 or 10 if benefics are deposited and aspected by benefics they will come to a compromise and the war will end in a treaty. But if the Kenthra houses are occupied by the malefics and also aspected by malefics war will go on to the last.

Sloka 5: If in the second or third house, Jupiter or Venus or both are deposited, the enemy will go back very soon. Under the same circumstances, anyone who would have gone abroad, will return back soon without any doubt.

CHAPTER IV

Sloka 1: If evil planets do not occupy 1, 4, 7, 8 or 10 the benefics are in 1, 4, 5, 7, 9 or 10 one will realise first ambition. If not, the result will be just opposite.

Sloka 2: There is success or progress if in the 3rd or 5th or 6th or 11th or 12th benefics are deposited. If the ascendant falls in Gemini, Virgo, or Aquarius and at the same time beneficial planets are also deposited therein, one is to expect desirable results.

Sloka 3: Improvement in status and achievement of one's aims are promised by beneficial planets, occupying the houses 7 and 10. If they are in the 1st or 2nd or 5th one can expect prestige, power and money. Evil planets occupying the 11th or the 12th cannot reduce any advantageous results. Moon in the ascendant does no good. But if it is 10th house, it is beneficial.

Sloka 4: If Jupiter aspects Moon deposited in 2 or 3 or 6 or 7 or 10 or 11 one can be happy, make gains, etc. through other sex. But evil planets in 1 or 3 or 5 or 8 or 9 bring loss, cause fear and also failure in one's attempts or business. But benefics in these houses are advantageous.

Sloka 5: Beneficial planets in I or 5 or 7 or 8 receiving aspect from other benefics and moon in Upachayasthana promises recovery from disease if the person is ill.

CHAPTER V

Person in far off place.

Sloka 1: If all planets occupy 2, 3 and 5 the person will return home early. If benefics occupy these houses the person whom it would have been thought to be lost, will return and also the missing article will be recovered. If Jupiter and Venus occupy the above 2, 3 or 5 the person will return shortly.

Sloka 2: The person will return home early if Jupiter is in a Kendra sign and if planet occupies the 6th or the 7th house. If Venus and Mercury were to be in 5 or 9 the person will return quickly.

Sloka 3: Moon in the 8th house and Kendra sign unoccupied by evil planets show that the person will return safely. Also he will return with gains if in the Kendra signs benefics are found.

Sloka 4: If the ascendant is a Prishtodaya sign and evil planets aspect it, the person is undergoing lot of trouble anp may be imprisoned. If evil planets are in the third house and they are not aspected by beneficial ones, that person would have started from his place to a foreign land. If in the sixth house, evil planets are deposited the person will be either dead or missing. He would have been kidnapped by robbers if the malefics are in the Kendra signs (1, 4, 7 or 10).

Sloka 5: Note the planet which is in direct motion next to the lagna; calculate how many rasis away from lagna it is. Multiply it by 12. In so many days he will return; if the planet next to lagna is retrograde, then he will return in as many days as the number of signs in between the ascendant and the sign occupied by the retrograde planet.

CHAPTER VI

To Regain The Lost

Sloka 1: A person related to the consultant alone, would have taken the missing article and it is there only, if the lagna falls in a fixed sign or the Navamsa lagna is in a fixed sign or if the lagna is Vargothama

Sloka 2: The article which is considered to be lost, will be near the gate of the house, if the first 10° of a sign will be the lagna. But if the lagna is between 10 and 20, the missed article will be in the middle portion of the house; if the lagna falls between 20 and 30, it will be in the backyard of the house.

Sloka 3: Strong Moon in lagna or the lagna is seershodhaya, occupied or aspected by beneficial planets, indicates that one will recover the property, considered to be lost. If the 11th house is occupied by a strong benefic, the property will be recovered.

Sloka 4: Planets situated in the houses 1, 4, 7 or 10 indicate where the property that had been missed, would be.. It indicates the direction by which it is taken. If there is no planet in the Kendra house, then the lagna indicates the direction. The distance by which the missing article is taken is to be ascertained by considering the Navamsa lagna. The nature of the property lost is judged through the navamsa lagna:

(a) the rising decanate describes the robbers.

(b) the ascendant indicates the time, when it was lost and also the direction by which and the place to which the missing article is taken. The age, caste, and other particulars of the robber is judged from the lord of the ascendant.

CHAPTER VII

Sloka 1: Predict that a male child will be born if Saturn is deposited in the houses 3, 5, 7, 9 or 11. If not, a girl will be born. As regards marriage, if Saturn is in even houses, one will be successful, otherwise, no.

Sloka 2: If Moon is in 3 or 5 or 6 or 7 or 11 receiving aspect from Jupiter and Mercury, marriage will take place. If benefics occupy Trikona or Kendra houses, then also marriage will come up.

Sloka 3: If Venus is 7 to Moon and Saturn is in 7 to Sun or if Venus and Saturn are in 4th and 8th houses, there will be the rain during the rainy season.

Sloka 4: If benefics are found in the houses 1, 2, 3, 4, 7 or 10 and such a house is a watery one, there will be rain. If Moon is in the ascendant in a watery sign predict that there will be the rain.

Sloka 5: A male child will be born if the lagna be in a masculine sign and also aspected by male planets. If the ascendant be a female sign, receiving aspect from feminine planets, the child will be female. If Mercury is situated in the ascendant, the lady will be pregnant.

Sloka 6: One would like to know about a girl if Moon or Mercury either occupies or aspects the lagna. If Saturn. occupies or aspects the lagna, the query will be about an old woman. If Jupiter or Sun is situated in the ascendant or if they aspect the ascendant, the lady in question would have recently delivered. The lady will be maintaining strong health if Mars or Venus occupies or aspects the ascendant. The above principle is also applicable for males.

Sloka 7: Strong planet in the lagna indicates that the consultant would like to know about one with whom he is closely connected. If the strong planet is in the third house, the matter will be about one's youngster. If it occupies the 5th.it relates to one's offspring. If the strong planet is in 4, the matter relates to the mother or sister. If it is in 6 the query is about the enemy.

Sloka 8: If it is in 7, about one's wife, if in 9, it will be about a virtuous person doing meritorious deed. If it is in 10 it relates to one's Guru. If the Lord of the Navamsa lagna is strong in the Horary lagna one will put questions about himself. If the Lord of the Navamsa lagna is strong and is a friend to the Lord of the Horary Lagna, the question will be about one's friend. If they are enemical, the question will be about one's enemy.

Sloka 9: If the lagna falls in a movable sign and also the navamsa lagna, the question will be about one who has left his place and gone overseas. If a planet has moved away from the 7th house and it is not retrograde, he, who has gone out, will return. But if the planet is retrograde he will not return.

Sloka 10: One will enjoy another man's wife if Sun. Venus or Mars is situated in the 7th house. One will enjoy his own wife if the 7th house is occupied by Jupiter. One enjoys a prostitute if Moon or Mercury is in 9. One enjoys a woman of a very low caste if Saturn is in 7. From the nature of the Moon, ascertain the age of the woman.

Sloka 11: He who has gone out will fall ill in a foreign land if in the 9th house Saturn and some other evil planet are deposited. He who has gone out will not return, but will die there itself, if Saturn is in 8.

Sloka 12: One's father who had already left his place will further move on to another country if Sun or a benefic or both are in the 8th house, receiving aspect from another benefic.

Thus Prithuyasas has given various principles in his book, Shatpanchasika".

Will he be chaste

If the sublord of the 7th or the planet in whose constellation the sublord is deposited is neither Mercury nor one occupying a dual sign and also if the sublord of the 11th is in the constellation of a planet signifying the 7th house, the person will remain the chaste. When the sublord of the 11th is in the constellation of the planet signifying the 6th even thought one may be moving with the other sex, yet the other sex will not permit to proceed beyond a particular limit and thereby the person has to necessarily remain chaste.

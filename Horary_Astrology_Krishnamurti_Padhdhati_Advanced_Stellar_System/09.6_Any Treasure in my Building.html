Any Treasure in my Building

If the sub-lord of the 4th cusp is deposited in a constellation. lord of which is the significator of the houses 2 or 11 or 6 one will surely have the treasure. Further the lord of the constellation should have any connection with Saturn in direct motion. According to traditional astrology if lord of 2 were to be in the 8th house, one can come by treasure or if lords of 2, 4, 9 and 11 conjoin, one have treasure. But in Horary astrology it is the sub-lord of the 4th house which will give a correct picture, when the question relates to Treasure.

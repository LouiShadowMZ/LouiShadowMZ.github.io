WHICH TIME IS GOOD

(a) To have a servant:

You can ask your servant to start his work at a time when the ascendant rising in the East is not afflicted by the significators of the 8th and 12th houses. But, if you appoint him at a time when the sub-lord of the ascendant in the significator of 6 and 11, you will surely have satisfaction.

(b) To buy an article:

The sub-lord of the second house must be deposited in the constellation of a planet which is the significator of 6 and 11.

(c) To sell an article:

If the sub-lord of the 12th must be situated in the constellation, the lord of which is the significator of the houses 6 and 11.

(d) To enter into service:

The sub-lord of the sixth is to be deposited in the constella. tion of the planet signifying houses 10 and 11.

(e) To sign an agreement:

The sub-lord of the third cusp is to be deposited in a constellation, the lord of which is the significator of the houses 6 and 11.

(f) To start a business:

The sub-lord of the 7th is to be deposited in the constellation the lord of which is the significator of the houses 6 and 11.

(g) To release a film:

The sub-lord of the 7th is to be deposited in the constellation, the lord of which is the significator of 6 and 11.

(h) To lay foundation:

The sub-lord of the fourth is to be deposited in the constellation of the significator of the 11th and not a significator of 5 and 12.

(i) Will I go overseas for probation or on promotion?

If the sub-lord of the 6th cusp is deposited in the constellation of a planet which is the significator of 9 and 12, you go overseas, only for probation, training or education. But, if the sub-lord of the 6th cusp is deposited in a constellation, the lord of which is a significator of the 11th house and also either 6 or 10, you are sure to have promotion.

(g) Whom to pray:

Astrologically, while judging a horoscope, note the planets which are very advantageous to fulfil one's desire; then make a selection of a particular form of god or Goddess or any low form like yekshini, Saththan, Kurali, etc. But my sincere advice would be to pray to your Guru, the preceptor who puts you on the way and removes your darkness. It is said "Gurur Brahma, Gurur Vishnu, Gurur Devoh Mahaeswara." Hence in Guru you can find all the three; to produce anything you desire. to protect anything you want and to get rid of those which you wish. Hence I strongly recommend to have the blessings of your Guru. It will be much more effective, if you receive the blessings of Jagadguru, who never cared for his wordly pleasures, but took Sanyasin life in youth and daily spends most of the hours in doing Pooja to the idol which was worshipped for hundreds of years and who does Japa for hours. Further the people with whom he spends most of the remaining time are those who are religious or try to be religious, Godfearing, etc.. Such Gurus are considered as the incarnation of God to guide us and put us in the right path. People who have the fortune in the future alone, that is, those who have done meritorious deed in the previous birth alone can have such a Dharsan. All cannot have the chances to visit his place. Such of those can have the Dharsan of his disciples.

Today in India if anybody has the desire to meet such a Guruji and at the same time, one has neither time nor money, then they can simply pray to Him having the following photo. He is our Jagadguru, Kanchikamakoti Peetadhipathi Shri Sankarachariar Swamigal Avargal.

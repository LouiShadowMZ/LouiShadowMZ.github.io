Where Does This Magick Come From?

I never share magick unless I have found it to work for myself and others. I don't mind whether magick is ancient or modern, so long as it works. The magick presented in this book is partly modern, but the spirits and entities that are employed have been around for a long time.

除非我发现某个魔法对我自己和他人有效，否则我从不分享。我不介意魔法是古老的还是现代的，只要它有效。本书中呈现的魔法部分是现代的，但所使用的灵体和实体已经存在了很长时间。

Just about any spell or ritual you want can be found on the Internet with a little bit of searching, but magick that works is a little more rare.

只要稍加搜索，几乎任何你想要的咒语或仪式都可以在互联网上找到，但有效的魔法则更为罕见。

Although ancient grimoires are now easily obtained online, most of these books leave out the important instructions for their use.

尽管古老的魔典现在很容易在网上获得，但这些书中的大多数都省略了其使用的重要说明。

One of the tasks of modern occultists is to illuminate the process behind the words. If you were to obtain a copy of The Black Screech Owl, you could find some of the magickal words included in this book. But that is all you would find. I know of several people who have tried the instructions in that book, treating the instruction literally. The magick does not work. However, when you unravel the secrets in the text, you find how to actually work the magick.

现代神秘学者的任务之一就是阐明言辞背后的过程。如果你能得到一本《黑尖枭》（The Black Screech Owl）的副本，你可能会在本书中找到一些魔法词汇。但那也就是你能找到的全部了。我知道有几个人曾尝试过那本书中的说明，完全按照字面意思理解。魔法并不起作用。然而，当你解开文本中的秘密时，你会发现如何实际运用魔法。

That is what I will give you here. Then secret to making ancient magick work in the modern world.

这就是我将在这里给你的。让古老魔法在现代世界发挥作用的秘密。

In that sense, there is almost nothing in this book that I have intend from scratch. But this book will communicate to you exactly how to preform the rituals, what to think, feel and how to get the results you want.

从这个意义上说，这本书里几乎没有什么是我从头开始构想的。但是这本书会准确地告诉你如何执行仪式，思考什么，感受什么，以及如何得到你想要的结果。

Magick Works

Magick works, but it doesn't always work the way you expect. I once performed an elaborate ritual to find a low-cost house, and as soon as it was done a relative offered to sell me a house with $100,000 knocked off the price. Not what I expected, but a result, and a good result at that. So be aware of money turning up in unexpected ways.

魔法确实有效，但它并不总是按照你期望的方式运作。我曾经举行过一个复杂的仪式来寻找一栋低价的房子，仪式一结束，一位亲戚就主动提出以低于市价10万美元的价格卖给我一栋房子。并非我所期望的，但却是一个结果，而且是一个好结果。所以要注意金钱以意想不到的方式出现。

But what if it doesn't work? You may have asked for too much or too little, or you may just need more practice.

但如果它不起作用呢？你可能要求得太多或太少，或者你只是需要更多练习。

A good trick to get the magick working is this: use the Cashbook to attract something really small, but incredibly specific. So, if you need $500, just forget that for now. Instead, try requesting an obscure amount such as 50c or $3. Something that feels so easy it's almost pointless to do magick for.

让魔法起作用的一个好技巧是：用现金簿吸引一些非常小但极其具体的东西。所以，如果你需要500美元，现在就忘了它。相反，尝试请求一个不起眼的金额，比如50美分或3美元。一些感觉如此容易以至于几乎没有必要为其施行魔法的东西。

This works, because it's such a small amount, and then your disbelief falls away. You let go of all lust for result, because who cares about 50c anyway? But when this specific amount turns up out of the blue, within a few days, it lets you know that magick works. Even though it's for a tiny amount, it gives you more trust in the magick. It's an easy and fun way to get things started but think of it as an enjoyable way to do magick, rather than that you are testing the magick. The moment you test magick, you're lacking trust, and that can short-circuit everything. (Remember also that the Cashbook often throws in a little extra on top of what you request. So, if you ask for a very specific $3, don't be surprised if you get $3.30.)

这之所以有效，是因为金额如此之小，然后你的怀疑就会消失。你放下了所有对结果的渴求，因为谁会在乎50美分呢？但是当这个特定的金额在几天内凭空出现时，它会让你知道魔法是有效的。即使只是为了很小的金额，它也会让你更加信任魔法。这是一种轻松有趣的方式来启动事情，但要把它看作是一种享受魔法的方式，而不是你在测试魔法。当你测试魔法的那一刻，你就缺乏信任，这可能会使一切短路。（还要记住，现金簿通常会在你请求的金额之上额外增加一点。所以，如果你请求非常具体的3美元，不要惊讶你得到3.30美元。）

The Cashbook works well for most people if the instructions are followed closely, but if you're having trouble getting started, give this a try and it should get things flowing. Money finds a way.

如果严格按照说明操作，现金簿对大多数人都很有效，但如果你在起步时遇到困难，试试这个方法，它应该能让事情顺利进行。金钱总能找到出路。

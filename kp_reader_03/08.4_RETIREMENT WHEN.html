RETIREMENT – WHEN?

Houses 2, 6 & 10 indicate service. So 12th house to the above i.e 1, 5 & 9 show end of service or retirement.

Signification of 1, 5, 9

Saturn is in 1 and Mercury is in sat star. Sun is owner of 1 and no planets in sun star.

No planet in 5. Jupiter is the owner. Mars is in Jupiter Star.

Moon & Ketu are in 9 - No body in Moon Star. Venus & Jupiter are in Ketu star.

Rahu also to he taken as Significater as it represent Venus and also aspected by Saturn & Moon.

So all the nine planets come as significators. Remember nodes are stronger than planets.

At the time of retirement (normally around 60 year of age) he will me running Jupiter dasa Saturn Bhukti (from 28.5.79). Among the significators, Ketu is the strongest and hence choose Ketu Anthra.

So at the time when sun transits in the sign governed by Jupiter (dasa Lord) in the star Uthrapadra (Bukthi Lord) and Ketu Sub (Anthara lord), on or around 21-3-1980 he will retire from service.

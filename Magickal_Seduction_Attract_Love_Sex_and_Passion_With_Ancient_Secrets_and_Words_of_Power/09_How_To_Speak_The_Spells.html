How To Speak The Spells

Most occultists spend too much time worrying about pronunciation. What follows is sometimes English and partly Latin, along with words of many other origins. Everything here can be pronounced in many ways, depending on how you interpret Latin, your native language and your accent. There is no correct pronunciation. I have however, included a phonetic pronunciation beneath the words in Parenthesis. This pronunciation will work.

大多数神秘学者花费太多时间担心发音。接下来是部分英语和部分拉丁语，以及许多其他来源的词语。这里的每样东西都可以用多种方式发音，具体取决于你如何理解拉丁语、你的母语和你的口音。没有正确的发音。然而，我在括号中的词语下方加入了一个音标发音。这个发音会有效。

So, where it says 'Descendimus ab aere', you would pronounce this as DESK-END-IM-US AB AIR.

所以，当它说 ‘Descendimus ab aere’ 时，你会把它读成 DESK-END-IM-US AB AIR。

Many people would pronounce these words differently. I often employ the help of a local University professor to assist with my Latin, but even he suggests many different versions, so what follows is not conventional or even consistent. But the pronunciation shown here has worked for me and others, so it can be fully trusted.

许多人会以不同的方式念出这些词。我经常请一位当地大学的教授协助我处理拉丁文，但他甚至也建议了许多不同的版本，所以接下来的内容既不传统也不一致。但是这里展示的发音对我和其他人都是有效的，所以完全可以信赖。

More important than the pronunciation is that way you let the words come out of your body. Let the words vibrate from the base of your lungs, through your throat and out of your mouth as though you are speaking them to the horizon. They should not be whispered, but allowed to resonate in your throat as they pass out of you.

比发音更重要的是你让词语从你身体里出来的方式。让词语从你的肺部底部震动，通过你的喉咙，然后从你的嘴里出来，就好像你正对着地平线说话一样。它们不应该是耳语，而应该允许在你的喉咙里共鸣，当它们从你身体里出来的时候。

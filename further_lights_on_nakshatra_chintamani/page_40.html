The 7th cusp is jointly ruled by Jupiter (sign lord), Ketu (star lord) and Venus (sub lord). Jupiter is in the star Mercury and in the sub Venus. Ketu is in the star Venus and in the sub Mars. Venus is in the star Venus and in the sub Mercury. So the joint rulers of the 7th cusp are controlled by Venus, Mars and Mercury respectively as their sub lords.

Venus owns 6, Mars owns 12 and Mercury owns 1. Venus and Mercury are retrograde. They both are surrounded by the malefics Ketu and Saturn (owner 10). So they in the last instance indicate negation of married life. Hence the possibility of separation or divorce.

Example Nativities - 25.Industrialist

Hints: 1. If the sub lord of the 7th cusp (business, industry) be the significator (in the star of the occupant or owner) of 2, 10 or 11; or connected with 2, 10 or 11 the native gains in business or industry during the joint period of the significators of 2, 10 and 11.

2.If the sub lord of the 10th cusp (profession, vocation, occupation) be the significator (in the star of the occupant or owner) of 2, 7 or 11; or connected with 2, 7 or 11 the native gets success in an independent profession, vocation or occupation during the joint period of the significators 2, 7 and 11.

3 (a) If the sub lord of the 2nd or 11th cusp be the significator (in the star of the occupant or owner) of 2, 6 or 11; or connected with 2, 6 or 11 the native gains wealth during the joint period of the significators of 2, 6 and 11.

(b) If it be the significator (in the star of the occupant or owner) only of 8, 12 or 5 (being 2nd, 6th and 11th from the 7th i.e. gain to the opposite party) money is lost during the joint period of the significators of 8 and 12. (Note: Page 13, line 20, column III read 8, 12 instead of 8, 12, 5)

4.If the sub lord of the 7th cusp (business, partnership, industry) be the significator (in the star of the occupant or owner) of 8 or 12 the native suffers loss of money in business, industry or partnership transactions during the joint period of the significators of 8 and 12.

5.In judging the nature of profession, vocation or occupation consider the joint rulers of the 10th cusp alongwith their star lords and sub lords. Note the signs occupied by them, their association and aspects formed by them. One should be acquainted with the characteristic features of signs and planets in respect of vocations or occupations. Discretion has to be used in determining the nature of vocation or occupation.

Note the period ruler and the sub period ruler at the time of judgment, because the sub period rulers denote changes in vocations at times in many cases.

1.7th cusp (Virgo 1°30'): In this horoscope Pisces 1°30' is on the Asc. The sub lord of the 7th cusp is Jupiter. It is in its own star and sub. It owns 1 (native), 10 (vocation, fame, status) and 11 (success, fulfilment of aspirations and ambitions). Thus the cuspal sub lord of 7 is the significator of 1, 10 and 11. Hence the native will gain in business or industry during the joint period of the significators of 2, 10 and 11.

2.2nd cusp (Aries 8°14′): The sub lord of the 2nd cusp is Jupiter. So as discussed above the native will acquire wealth during the joint period of the significators of 2, 6 and 11.

11th cusp (Sagittarius 28°14'): The sub lord of the 11th cusp is Moon. It is in the star Mercury and in the sub Ketu. Its star lord Mercury owns 4, 5, 7 and occupies 11. Its sub lord Ketu being in Pisces denotes the results of 1, 10, and 11 as a representative of Jupiter. Moon owns 6. Thus the cuspal sub lord of 11 is the significator of 11 (success), 5 (enterprise), 4 (immovable estates), 7 (partnership business or industry) and connected with 1 (self-help), 6 (efficiency, labour), 10 (activities, progress, ambitions) and 11 (fulfilment of desires). Hence the native will get success in industrial activities, partnership enterprises, immovable estates and in his missions, during the joint period of the significators of 2, 6 and 11.

3.10th cusp (Sagittarius 3°14'): 1. The sub lord of the 10th cusp is Sun, It is in the star Venus and in the sub Jupiter.

Sun is in the fire sign Sagittarius. The star lord Venus is in the water sign Scorpio owned by Mars. The sub lord Jupiter occupies 8 in Libra owned by Venus.

Water signs and Venus denote oil. Fire signs and Mars signify chemicals. Venus aspected by Saturn (minerals) indicates mineral oils. Mars aspected by Jupiter supports chemicals.Jupiter in the 8th corresponds to Scorpio (chemicals), it being in Libra corresponds to Venus (oil). Thus Venus in Scorpio and Jupiter in the 8th in Libra denote chemical oils. Hence the cuspal sub lord of 10 signifies the industry of chemical oils. The native owns his factory for manufacturing chemical oils.

2.Sun occupies 10. Its star lord Venus owns 3 (abilities) and 8 (sudden gain), occupies 9 (fortune). Its sub lord Jupiter owns 1, 10 and 11. Hence the native by his abilities will build his fortune, he will flourish in his industrial enterprise of chemical oils, he will acquire a covetable position in the society and he will meet with success by sudden gains.

4.Peculiarities: In this horoscope Ketu in Pisces represents Jupiter owning 1, 10 and 11. Rahu in Virgo represents Mercury owning 4, 5 and 7.

Sun, Jupiter, Saturn and Rahu are in the sub Jupiter. Moon and Mercury are in the sub Ketu i.e. Jupiter. Ketu is in the sub Rahu i.e. Mercury. Mars is in the sub Mercury. These planets are finally controlled by the owners of Kendra-Trikon. Further these sub lords Mercury and Jupiter are not aspected by any malefic. Hence the combined effect of these planets is very beneficial in general.

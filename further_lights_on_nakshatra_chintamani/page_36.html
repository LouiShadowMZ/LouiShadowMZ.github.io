2.The star lord Mars (drugs) is in Leo (medicine). The sub lord Jupiter (medical line) is in Scorpio (drugs) and aspected by Mars. 3.The function of an anaesthetist is to make insensible the part of the body of a patient to be operated. So in the horoscope of a doctor Sun represents life or liveliness. Mercury stands for consciousness or sensibility. Rahu, Ketu and Saturn signify unconsciousness or insensibility. Hence Sun or Mercury is connected with Rahu, Ketu and Saturn in the horoscope of an anaesthetist.

If it is the significator of 1, 4 or 10 (being 12th from 2, 5 and 11) child birth is not promised.

5th cusp (Virgo 6° 12'): In this horoscope Taurus 17°44′ is on the Asc. The sub lord of the 5th cusp is Mercury. It is in the star Mars and in the sub Saturn.

Mercury owns 2, 5 and occupies 5; it aspects the 11th. So it is connected with 2, 5 and 11. Hence child birth is promised.

Mercury is associated with Ketu and aspected by Saturn. Hence delay in child birth.

The star lord Mars owns the maraka houses 7, 12 and occupies 6; it aspects the 12th and Asc. The sub lord Saturn occupies 8; it aspects the 2nd and 5th, also Ketu and Mercury in 5. So this cuspal sub lord is connected with Mars (operation), Saturn (obstruction), Ketu (unnatural), the houses 1 (native), 6 (sickness), 8 (danger, operation), 12 (hospitalisation); 2, 5 (child birth). Hence the possibility of Caesarean

11th cusp (Pisces 6°12′): The sub lord of the 11th cusp is Mercury. Hence the position as above.

8th cusp (Sagittarius 12°12′): It denotes operation. So its sub lord may be considered. Its sublord is Mercury. Hence the possibility of caesarean.

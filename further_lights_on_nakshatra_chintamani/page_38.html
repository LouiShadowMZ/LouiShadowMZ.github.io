As the star lord Mars aspects the 7th and 11th the cuspal sub lord of 5 becomes the significator of 7 and 11. Hence marriage to a person of the love affair.

Joint period: The native was born during the period of Ketu with balance of 2Y. 3M. 12D.. She married the person of her love affair during the joint period of Sun, Saturn, Jupiter and Venus on 27-2-1960 in the foreign land. They all are connected with 2, 7, 11 (marriage) and 3, 9, 12 (foreign land).

The period ruler Sun is in the star and sub Rahu. Rahu is in 3, it is in association with the owner of 3 (Saturn), the owner of 7 and 12 (Venus); so it denotes the results of 7, 3 and 12. Hence Sun indicates marriage in the foreign land.

The sub period ruler Saturn is in the star Mars and in the sub Jupiter. The star lord Mars is in 3, aspects 7 and 11; it is in conjunction with the owner of 11 (Mercury). The sub lord Jupiter owns 2, occupies 11 and aspects the owner of 9 (Moon). So Saturn becomes the significator of 7, 11, 3 and connected with 2, 11 and 9. Hence Saturn denotes marriage in the foreign land.

The inter period ruler Jupiter is in the star Mars and in the sub Mercury. So as discussed above Jupiter is the significator of 7, 11 and 3. Hence marriage in the foreign land.

The sookshma period ruler Venus is in the star Moon and in the sub Saturn. The star lord Moon owns 9 and the sub lord Saturn owns and occupies 3. Further, as there is no planet in the star of Venus, Venus is the significator of 2, 7, 12 and also of 3 and 9. Hence marriage in the foreign land.

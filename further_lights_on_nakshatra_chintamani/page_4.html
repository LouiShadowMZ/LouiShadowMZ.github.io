ASC (Aquarius 29° 8'): In this horoscope the sub lord of the Asc, is Sun (spirit). It is in the star Venus and in the sub Rahu. The star lord Venus owns 8 (spiritualism) and 3 (mental qualities); it is in the water sign (devotion) Pisces (renunciation). The sub lord Rahu occupies 12 (renunciation), it is in conjunction with the owner of 9 (morals, religion, philosophy) Mars. Sun is in the fire sign Aries (self restraint); it is aspected by Saturn the recluse owning 1 and 12. Thus the sub lord of the Asc, is connected with 3, 8, 12 and Saturn; also the signs of spiritual qualities. Hence the native has given up worldly ties and interests.

11th cusp (Sagittarius 26° 42'): The sub lord of the 11th cusp is Sun. Hence the spiritual attainment as discussed.

Period: The native was born during the period of Ketu with balance of 5Y. 5M. 28D. Thereafter the native has to run the periods of Venus, Sun, Moon, Mars etc. All the planets are the significators of spiritual attainment. Hence the native has from her early age given up worldly ties and interests. She is a saint. She is shri Anand mayiji of Bengal.

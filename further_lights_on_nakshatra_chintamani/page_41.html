Its sub lord Mercury is in 6 in conjunction with the owner of 10 (Jupiter) in Virgo. Thus the cuspal sub lord of 7 is strongly connected with 1 (absence of family life), 6 (absence of married life), 8 (disappointment), 10 (absence of permanent relationship) and also the barren sign. Hence the separation in married life.

Profession:

10th cusp (Sagittarius 27° 7'): The 10th cusp is jointly ruled by Jupiter (sign lord), Sun (star lord) and Sun (sub lord). Jupiter denotes law, it is in conjunction with Mercury (reason, speech, writings) in the critical sign Virgo. Sun indicates Government, it is in the regal sign Leo in conjunction with Mars (arguments) owning the Asc (native). So the native took to legal profession.

The cuspal sub lord of 10 Sun is in the star Ketu and in the sub Mercury. The star lord Ketu is in the 7th (courts, litigations), it is in the subtle sign Scorpio and aspected by Mars the owner of the Asc (native). The sub lord Mercury owns 3 (native's bent of mind), it is in conjunction with the owner of 9 (legal matters) and 10 (vocation, ambitions) Jupiter (law). Hence the native was practising as a solicitor.

Death:

The native died of heart attack on the way enroute to Kedarnath on 9-5-1975 during the joint period of Mercury (the cuspal sub lord of 8), Saturn (the badhaka owner of 11 and maraka owner of 12) and Sun (the cuspal sub lord of 6).

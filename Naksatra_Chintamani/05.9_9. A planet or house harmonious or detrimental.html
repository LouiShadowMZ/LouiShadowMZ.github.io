It will be clear from the above table that the houses to be considered for a particular subject matter are ‘harmonious‘ for that subject matter, e.g. the houses 2, 6 and 10 are harmonious to the matters of service and so on with other houses.

Similarly the houses which denote negation or denial of the matters of the houses to be considered are 'detrimental' to the houses to be considered, e.g. the houses 1, 5 and 9 being 12th from 2, 6 and 10 respectively denote negation of the matters signified by 2, 6 and 10. So the houses 1 ,5 and 9 are 'detrimental' to the houses 2, 6 and 10; and so on with other houses.

A planet harmonious or detrimental

1. A planet harmonious to any house：In view of the above table the occupant or owner of 2, 6 or 10，the planet in the star of the occupant or owner of 2, 6 or 10 and the planet in conj. or in aspect with the significator of 2, 6 or 10 which come under the category of the significators of 2, 6 and 10 are termed as harmonious to the houses 2, 6 and 10; and so on

2. A planet detrimental to any house：

  （a）The occupant or owner of 1, 5 or 9，the planet in the star of the occupant or owner of 1, 5 or 9 and the planet in conj. or in aspect with the significator of 1, 5 or 9 which come under the category of the significators of 1, 5 and 9 show negation of the matters signified by 2, 6 and 10. So the significator of 1, 5 and 9 are termed as detrimental to the houses 2, 6 and 10.

  （b）A planet in 6, 8 or 12 sign（house）as counted from its own sign（house）becomes detrimental to the matters of that house（owned by the planet）.

  （c）A planet placed in 6, 8 or 12 as counted from the house under consideration becomes detrimental to the matters of the house under consideration.

  （d）The owner of the house 6, 8 or 12 as counted from the house under consideration becomes detrimental to the matters of the house under consideration.

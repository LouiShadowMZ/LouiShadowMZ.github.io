COLOR ASSOCIATIONS IN WESTERN HORARY ASTROLOGY

Sometimes the colors associated with the planet signifying the missing item or with the astrological house or sign containing the significator can give clues to the itemâ€™s whereabouts, but the colors suggested in the literature should probably be taken with a grain of salt. The following lists of colors are based on comments by Lilly, Barclay, and Simmonite.

Colors Linked to the Signs

Aries: Blood red, fiery colors, white mixed with red, pink

Taurus: Red and citron mixture, white mixed with lemon, creamy

Gemini: Red and white mixture, pink

Cancer: Green or russet

Leo: Golden, orange, red, green

Virgo: Black with blue splotches, speckled, dark colors

Libra: Dark red, crimson, tawny, black, pastel shades

Scorpio: Dark brown, maroon, dark green

Sagittarius: Yellow, dark yellow, orange-yellow, green-sanguine (golden green)

Capricorn: Dark brown, russet, swarthy brown, black

Aquarius: Light blue, sky blue

Pisces: Pure white, glistening white

Colors Linked to the Traditional Planets

Sun: Golden, yellow, scarlet, red, purple

Moon: Pale, creamy, white, silver-white, mother-of-pearl, pale yellow-white, pale green

Mercury: Light blue, azure, gray, quicksilver, gray mixed with sky-color, multicolored

Venus: Light colors, white, sky-color mixed with brown or a little green

Mars: Fiery color, red, yellow, saffron, orange, scarlet

Jupiter: Bright, clear, azure, royal blue, indigo, purple, navy blue, red mixed with green, ashen

Saturn: Very dark in color, black, dark brown, dark green

Colors Linked to the Houses

First House: White, pale, wan, light gray

Second House: Green

Third House: Red and yellow, croceal, saffron, sorrel (light reddish brown, or chestnut-colored)

Fourth House: Red

Fifth House: Mixed black and white, honey, amber, golden, sanguine

Sixth House: Dark colors, black

Seventh House: Black

Eighth House: Green and black (dark green)

Ninth House: Green and white (light green)

Tenth House: Red and white (light red, pink)

Eleventh House: Saffron, yellow, red-orange (like the Sun)

Twelfth House: Green

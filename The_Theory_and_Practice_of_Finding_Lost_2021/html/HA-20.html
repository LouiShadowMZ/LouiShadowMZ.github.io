Chapter 20

PLANETARY KEYWORDS
OF VETTIUS VALENS (C. 175 CE)

The second-century Hellenistic astrologer Vettius Valens provided a list of planetary keywords dating back to the origins of Western astrology. The following is a summary of Valens’s planetary associations from his Anthology, translated by Mark Riley. Some items on this list may appear dated because they refer to the culture of Hellenistic Egypt, but it is valuable to become familiar with these associations because they are often helpful when interpreting horary charts.
